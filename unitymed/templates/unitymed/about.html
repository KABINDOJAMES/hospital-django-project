Unity medical Centre

Unity Medical Center is a clinic located in Kiunguro Ruiru along Thika road.The clinic have a dedicated focus on the provision of quality and unmatched healthcare services to the residents of Ruiru and its environs. We provide a range of medical and minor surgical services to the local Ruiru community as well as the neighbouring community's patients. The clinic offers 24 hours service to its customers in order to cater for the all the emergenicies that could arise at any time of the day. Along with the survices offerd at Unity Medical Center include; Outpatiennt services, General consultation, BP monitor, Diabetes clinic, Antenatal clinic, Minor surgeries,Circumcision,Gaenacological services,Dispensing, Pharmacy and Labaratory Services
